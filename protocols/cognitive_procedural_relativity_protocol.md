# Protocol: cognitive/procedural fee relativities

## Question
Do public reimbursement schedules structurally reward procedural care over cognitive work?

## Methods
Define baskets of specialist consultation/cognitive services and matched procedural services. Classify fee concepts, professional/facility bundling and currency/PPP assumptions. Report ratio ranges rather than unsupported point estimates when comparability is weak.

## Required review
Clinical service basket review, health-economic fee-concept review and sensitivity analyses for PPP, locality adjustment and bundled facility components.
