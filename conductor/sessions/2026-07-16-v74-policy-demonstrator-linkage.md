# v74 Policy demonstrator linkage

## Scope

Extend the first policy demonstrator to connect genomic schedule pricing and coverage restriction
signals without treating fixture records as item-level matches.

## Local evidence

- Two genomic schedule items are priced in the local fixture slice.
- Two genomic coverage decisions are present and marked covered with restrictions.
- The generated brief records both denominators and states that no equivalence or causal linkage
  is inferred.
- The dashboard exposes the linkage summary and the unit suite covers the contract.

## Boundary

This remains a synthetic, review-gated demonstrator. Real source bundles, licence review,
mapping adjudication and protocol approval are required before policy claims.
