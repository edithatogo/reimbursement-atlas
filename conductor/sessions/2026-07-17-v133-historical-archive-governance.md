# v133 historical MBS archive governance

## Scope

Advance historical MBS/PBS expansion without downloading or publishing licence-gated historical
payloads.

## Results

The official MBS downloads, 2010 to 2019 and previous-downloads indexes were rechecked. The
repository retains a metadata-only inventory of 343 targets across 32 archive pages, spanning
1974 through 2026. Issue #255 was updated with the inventory and source-term evidence.

## Decision

Keep every historical target `manual_review_only` and `public_reuse_review`. Historical metadata
is an acquisition-planning output, not evidence that a payload may be downloaded, parsed or
republished. No historical raw payloads were added to the repository.
